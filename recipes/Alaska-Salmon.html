Alaska Salmon

Description

Baked salmon makes an excellent main course!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 5 teaspoons honey

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Season each salmon fillet with salt and pepper
  3. Bake for 10 minutes per inch of thickness
